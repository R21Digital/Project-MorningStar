⚡ Force Sensitive Builds

Harness the power of the Force

⚠️ Note: Force Sensitive character slots are extremely rare and valuable. Choose your build path carefully as respeccing is difficult.
Jedi Guardian

Overview: Tank-focused Jedi with defensive abilities

Key Skills: Lightsaber 4/4/4/4, Force Defense 4/4/4/4

Best For: Group tanking, PvE content, defensive play

Jedi Consular

Overview: Force power specialist with utility abilities

Key Skills: Force Powers 4/4/4/4, Force Healing 4/4/4/4

Best For: Support role, group healing, utility

Sith Lord

Overview: Dark side focused on damage and fear

Key Skills: Lightsaber 4/4/4/4, Force Lightning 4/4/4/4

Best For: High damage output, intimidation, solo play

Hybrid Force User

Overview: Balanced light and dark side abilities

Key Skills: Mixed Force disciplines for versatility

Best For: Adaptive gameplay, exploring all Force aspects
