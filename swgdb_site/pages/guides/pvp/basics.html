⚔️ PvP Fundamentals

Essential PvP mechanics and strategies

Getting Started in PvP
  1. Flag for PvP: Use the /pvp command or visit a recruiter
  2. Choose a Faction: Join Imperial or Rebel forces
  3. Get Proper Gear: PSG armor, buffed weapons, medical supplies
  4. Learn the Basics: Movement, targeting, and terrain usage
Essential PvP Mechanics
  • Mind/Health Pools: Monitor both - death occurs when either reaches 0
  • States: Dizzy, stunned, and knocked down prevent actions
  • Range: Different weapons have optimal engagement distances
  • Cover: Use terrain and structures to break line of sight
  • Buffs: Essential for competitive play - maintain food/spice buffs
Combat Tips
  • Positioning: Control engagement distance based on your build
  • Target Priority: Focus healers and support players first
  • Resource Management: Don't waste action/mind on missed attacks
  • Escape Routes: Always plan your retreat before engaging
  • Group Coordination: Use voice comms and assist targets
Recommended Starting Builds
  • Rifleman/Medic: Good range, self-healing capability
  • TKA/Fencer: High mobility and burst damage
  • Carbineer/Medic: Balanced range and survivability
  • Master Doctor: Pure healer/support role
