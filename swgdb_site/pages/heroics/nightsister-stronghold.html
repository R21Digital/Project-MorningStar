    Strategy Guide

    Preparation

    Ensure all group members have poison resistance gear and Force resistance buffs. Bring plenty of healing supplies and Force power restoration items.

    Sister V'aria Fight

    Assign a dedicated healer to manage poison debuffs. Tanks should position V'aria away from the group to minimize AoE damage. DPS should focus on interrupting her Force Lightning casts.

    Matriarch Lishra Fight

    Prioritize killing elite guards quickly before they overwhelm the group. Assign interrupters to prevent ritual casting. Use crowd control abilities to manage mind-controlled players.

    Group Composition

    Recommended: 2 tanks, 2 healers, 4 DPS. Include at least one Force user for dispel abilities and one ranged DPS for safe positioning.
