    Strategy Guide

    Preparation

    Basic combat gear is sufficient. Bring standard healing supplies and any available buffs. This is a great opportunity to practice group coordination and communication.

    Phase 1 - Initial Engagement

    Start with basic positioning - tank in front, healers in back, DPS on the sides. Focus on killing blood collectors one at a time. Interrupt any blood rituals to prevent buffs.

    Phase 2 - Blood Leaders

    When blood leaders appear, prioritize killing them first to remove their leadership buffs. Use crowd control abilities to manage multiple targets. Coordinate interrupts on ritual casting.

    Phase 3 - Cleanup

    Focus on remaining enemies while maintaining group health. Use this time to practice coordination and communication. Ensure all group members understand their roles.

    Group Composition

    Recommended: 1 tank, 1 healer, 2 DPS. This is a great opportunity for new players to learn their roles. Include at least one player with interrupt abilities.

    Learning Opportunities

    Practice basic group mechanics like threat management, healing priorities, and damage coordination. Use this heroic to build confidence for more challenging content.
