Introduction

Star Wars Galaxies features a rich variety of questlines that provide story content, rewards, and character progression. These quests range from simple tutorial missions to complex multi-part storylines.

Quest Types: Quests in SWG can be categorized into story quests, profession quests, faction quests, heroic quests, and daily repeatable quests.

Quest Basics

  • Quest Givers: NPCs with quest indicators above their heads
  • Quest Log: Track active quests in your quest journal
  • Objectives: Clear objectives and waypoints guide your progress
  • Rewards: Experience, credits, items, and reputation gains

New Player Experience Questline

Welcome to the Galaxy
Level 1
Your first quest in SWG. Learn basic movement, interaction, and combat mechanics.
Rewards:
  • Basic Equipment Set
  • 100 Credits
  • 50 Experience Points
Steps:
  1. Talk to the Tutorial NPC
  2. Learn basic movement controls
  3. Practice combat with training droid
  4. Complete basic inventory tutorial
  5. Receive your first equipment
Choosing Your Path
Level 2
Learn about different professions and choose your first combat or crafting profession.
Rewards:
  • Profession Training
  • Basic Weapon
  • 200 Credits
  • 100 Experience Points
Steps:
  1. Visit profession trainers
  2. Learn about different professions
  3. Choose your first profession
  4. Complete basic training
  5. Receive profession-specific equipment
New Player Tips: Complete all tutorial quests before moving on to more advanced content. The rewards and experience will help you get started.

Heroic Quests

Heroic quests are challenging group content that require coordination and provide excellent rewards.

Heroic: The Death Watch Bunker
Level 30+
A challenging group quest to infiltrate a Mandalorian Death Watch bunker and recover valuable intelligence.
Rewards:
  • Rare Equipment
  • 5000 Credits
  • 2000 Experience Points
  • Faction Reputation
Steps:
  1. Form a group of 4-8 players
  2. Travel to the Death Watch location
  3. Fight through Mandalorian guards
  4. Defeat the bunker commander
  5. Recover the intelligence data
  6. Return to quest giver
Heroic Tips: Heroic quests require good coordination and communication. Make sure your group has a balanced composition with healers, tanks, and damage dealers.

Quest Tips

General Quest Tips

  • Read Quest Descriptions: Always read quest text carefully for important details
  • Check Requirements: Ensure you meet level and skill requirements before starting
  • Group Up: Some quests are easier or more fun with a group
  • Use Waypoints: Quest waypoints help guide you to objectives
  • Save Progress: Some quests can be paused and resumed later

Quest Efficiency

  • Quest Chains: Complete related quests together for better efficiency
  • Location Planning: Plan your quest route to minimize travel time
  • Resource Management: Bring appropriate supplies for longer quests
  • Backup Equipment: Have backup weapons and armor for challenging quests
Pro Tips

Use the quest journal to track multiple quests simultaneously. Some quests can be completed in parallel to maximize your efficiency.
