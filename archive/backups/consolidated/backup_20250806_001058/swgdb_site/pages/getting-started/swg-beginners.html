Getting Started

1

Download and Install SWG Restoration

Visit the official SWG Restoration website and download the client. Follow the installation instructions carefully.

Pro Tip

Make sure to run the launcher as administrator on Windows systems to avoid permission issues.

2

Create Your Account

Register for a free account on the SWG Restoration website. You'll need this to log into the game.

3

Choose Your Server

SWG Restoration typically has one main server. Connect and prepare to create your character.

SWG Restoration Login Screen

Character Creation

Starting Location

When you first log in, you'll be placed in a tutorial area. This is where you'll learn the basics of movement, interaction, and combat.

New Player Quest: The tutorial will guide you through basic controls, combat, and introduce you to your first profession trainer.

Character Customization

Take your time customizing your character's appearance. You can choose from various species, hairstyles, and clothing options.

Appearance Tips

Your character's appearance can be changed later at certain locations, so don't worry too much about getting it perfect the first time.

Combat Basics

Combat Types

SWG features three main types of combat:

  • Melee Combat: Close-range fighting with fists, swords, and other melee weapons
  • Ranged Combat: Using blasters, rifles, and other ranged weapons
  • Jedi Combat: Special force-based combat (requires specific conditions)

Combat Mechanics

Combat in SWG is based on dice rolls and statistics. Your character's skills, equipment, and buffs all affect combat performance.

Combat Tips

Always check your target's level before engaging. Higher-level enemies can be very difficult for new players.

Questing

Finding Quests

Quests can be found from NPCs throughout the galaxy. Look for NPCs with quest indicators above their heads.

Quest Types: There are story quests, daily quests, and repeatable quests. Story quests often provide the best rewards and experience.

Recommended Early Quests

  • Tutorial Quests: Complete all tutorial quests for basic equipment and experience
  • City Quests: Visit major cities for local quests and reputation gains
  • Profession Quests: Your profession trainer may offer special quests
Questing Tips

Always read quest descriptions carefully. Some quests have time limits or specific requirements.

Crafting

Crafting Basics

Crafting is a complex but rewarding system in SWG. You'll need to gather resources, learn schematics, and experiment to create better items.

Getting Started with Crafting

1

Choose a Crafting Profession

Start with a simple crafting profession like Tailor or Artisan to learn the basics.

2

Gather Resources

Use survey tools to find resources in the wilderness. Different areas have different resource types.

3

Learn Schematics

Purchase or find schematics to learn how to craft specific items.

Crafting Resources: Visit resource vendors in major cities to purchase basic crafting materials and tools.

Social Aspects

Joining the Community

SWG is a social game. Join guilds, participate in events, and make friends with other players.

Communication

Use the chat system to communicate with other players. Different chat channels serve different purposes:

  • General: General discussion and questions
  • Trade: Buying and selling items
  • Guild: Communication with your guild members
  • Group: Communication with your current group
Social Tips

Don't be afraid to ask for help! The SWG community is generally very helpful to new players.
