    Strategy Guide

    Preparation

    All players must have fire resistance gear and fear resistance buffs. Bring maximum healing supplies, Force power restoration items, and resurrection kits. Ensure all group members have completed prerequisite heroics.

    Phase 1 - Initial Engagement

    Start with careful positioning around the dragon. Assign multiple tanks to handle the massive damage. Healers should spread out to avoid AoE attacks. DPS should focus on consistent damage while avoiding tail swipes.

    Phase 2 - Young Krayts

    When young dragons appear, assign specific groups to handle them. Prioritize killing young dragons quickly before they overwhelm the group. Use crowd control abilities to manage multiple targets.

    Phase 3 - Burrowing

    When the dragon burrows, spread out to avoid emergence damage. Use defensive cooldowns during dragon breath attacks. Coordinate healing during fear phases to prevent group wipes.

    Group Composition

    Recommended: 3 tanks, 4 healers, 5 DPS. Include multiple Force users for fear resistance, at least two ranged DPS for safe positioning, and dedicated interrupters for dragon abilities.

    Key Mechanics

    Always spread out to minimize AoE damage. Use line of sight to avoid dragon breath. Coordinate defensive cooldowns during heavy damage phases. Young dragons must be killed quickly to prevent overwhelming the group.
