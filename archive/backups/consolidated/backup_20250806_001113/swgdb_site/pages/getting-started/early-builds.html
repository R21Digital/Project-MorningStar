Introduction

Choosing the right build for your first character is crucial for a good SWG experience. This guide covers builds that are:

  • Easy to Learn: Simple mechanics and straightforward progression
  • Self-Sufficient: Can handle most content without heavy reliance on others
  • Versatile: Good for various types of gameplay
  • Effective: Strong performance in their intended role

Build Selection Factors

Consider these factors when choosing your first build:

  • Playstyle: Do you prefer combat, crafting, or exploration?
  • Group vs Solo: Will you play mostly alone or with others?
  • Learning Curve: How much time do you want to spend learning?
  • Long-term Goals: What do you want to achieve in the game?

Progression Guide

Early Game (Levels 1-20)

  • Focus: Learn basic mechanics and choose your first profession
  • Goals: Complete tutorial quests and establish your build foundation
  • Equipment: Use basic equipment and focus on learning

Mid Game (Levels 20-50)

  • Focus: Master your build and expand your capabilities
  • Goals: Complete profession quests and improve equipment
  • Equipment: Upgrade to better weapons and armor

Late Game (Level 50+)

  • Focus: Optimize your build and tackle challenging content
  • Goals: Heroic quests, PvP, and advanced crafting
  • Equipment: Best-in-slot items and specialized gear
Progression Tips

Don't rush your progression. Take time to learn your build's mechanics and experiment with different approaches. The journey is as important as the destination.
