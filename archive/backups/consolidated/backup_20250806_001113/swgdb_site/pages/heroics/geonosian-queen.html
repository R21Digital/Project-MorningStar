    Strategy Guide

    Preparation

    Bring acid resistance gear and insectoid resistance buffs. Include area damage abilities to handle swarms. Ensure all players have ranged weapons for safe positioning.

    Phase 1 - Initial Engagement

    Start by clearing royal guards first to reduce the queen's protection. Use area damage abilities to handle worker swarms. Focus on interrupting the queen's egg laying abilities.

    Phase 2 - Swarm Management

    When the queen summons large swarms, prioritize killing eggs before they hatch. Use crowd control abilities to manage multiple targets. Assign specific players to handle royal guard respawns.

    Phase 3 - Final Stand

    Below 25% health, the queen becomes more aggressive. Focus all damage while managing remaining swarms. Use defensive cooldowns during acid spit phases.

    Group Composition

    Recommended: 1 tank, 2 healers, 3 DPS. Include at least one player with area damage abilities, one ranged DPS for safe positioning, and a dedicated interrupter for queen abilities.

    Key Mechanics

    Always destroy eggs quickly before they hatch. Use line of sight to avoid acid spit. Coordinate interrupts on the queen's abilities. Royal guards should be killed first to reduce the queen's protection.
